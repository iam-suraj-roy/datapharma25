Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and how we help you turn data into powerful business outcomes—before we even get started.

1. Can you help with statistical analysis for my thesis or dissertation?

Absolutely! We specialize in supporting academic projects and can assist with statistical methods, data interpretation, and visualization for theses, dissertations, and research reports across disciplines.

2. What kind of students do you work with?

We work with PhD scholars, master's students, and postgraduates from fields like MBA, MBBS, MD, Engineering, Pharmacy, and more—offering tailored support based on your subject and research requirements.

3. Can you assist with tools like SPSS, R, Python, or Excel?

Yes, we are proficient with a range of tools including SPSS, R, Python, Excel, Stata, and more. We’ll use the tool that best fits your project or the one your university prefers.

4. How do I share my data and requirements with you?

Once you get in touch, we will guide you to share your raw data, research objectives, or survey results securely. You can email us or use cloud services like Google Drive or Dropbox.

5. Is my data kept confidential?

Yes. Your privacy is very important to us. We treat every academic project with complete confidentiality and never share your data or results with anyone.
